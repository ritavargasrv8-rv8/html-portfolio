The Best Movies & Shows According To Me

Rita's Favorite Movies & Shows of All Time

The Hunger Games Series

I love how accurate the movies are to the books, except for the everything related to Peeta Melark. They really did not give him justice. Regardless, the movies are great! The acting, the scripts, the direction, the action, the drama, the grief were all presented well.

The Harry Potter Series

I've been a Potterhead since I was a kid. I've read the books, watched the movies, and even purchased some merches. I love the magical world! I love how it helped me escape reality for a really long time of my life.

The only thing that ruined Harry Potter for me was JK Rowling's transphobic comments. I was disappointed and it made me lose a lot of respect for her. I still love the books and movies but I can't support her anymore.

Game Of Thrones

Great Start. Terrible Ending. I just pretend Season 6 and beyond does not exist. Hey, let me live that fantasy, okay?

The Lord of the Rings Series

I've only watched the movies, I haven't read the books yet, but based on the feedback I've read online, the movies are pretty accurate. I really enjoyed them! I like anything that takes me away from reality and into a new world of adventures and magic.

Rita's Comfort Movies & Shows

People may argue that these aren't the best but they do bring me a sense of comfort and nostalgia.

Little Women (1994 and 2019 adaptations)

I love both adaptations. They both did a great job in capturing the essence of the story. Four girls-turned-women, with different dreams, different personalities, and different views in life, live and laugh and cry together. The story shows us that there is no one way to live life. There is no one way to be happy, and there is no one way to look at the world. We are all different, and we are all worthy no matter what. I love this story so much.

Friends

"It's like you're always stuck in second gear" - Yep, I feel that way a lot of times. It hasn't been my day, my week, my month, or even my year. But this show has always been there for me.

Parks & Recreation

The characters are funny and lovable. I love Leslie Knope the most! Her passion and dedication to her job, and to her goals of making a better world for everyone is utterly impressive. I know it's just a show, and she's just a fictional character, but I hope we have more of her in this real world we live in.
